## 場景一：高效便捷的引導分診
圖像輸入： 請上傳您醫院大廳的清晰照片作為背景參考圖。
文字提示詞 (Prompt)：
```
Reference the provided image of the hospital lobby for architectural style, lighting, and overall color palette.
Ultra-realistic, cinematic short film.
A middle-aged woman (patient) looking slightly confused, enters the lobby.
A sleek, human-like robot (Nurse-Bot) with a gentle, glowing blue chest panel gracefully approaches the woman.
The robot extends a hand to gently guide her arm towards a digital kiosk, which immediately displays her appointment details.
The robot nods reassuringly.
Smooth dolly shot following the robot's movement, ending in a medium close-up on the patient's relieved and grateful face.
Maintain the realistic textures and light from the reference image.
#SmartHospital, #RoboticGuidance, #Efficient, #FutureMedicine, #4K, #Cinematic, #WarmLighting
```

### 場景二：細緻入微的康復護理
圖像輸入： 請上傳您醫院康復室或治療室的清晰照片作為背景參考圖。
文字提示詞 (Prompt)：
```
Reference the provided image of the hospital physiotherapy room for room layout, natural light and equipment placement.
High-detail rendering.
A young male patient performing light stretching exercises in the foreground.
The same human-like robot, acting as a physical therapist assistant, is precisely adjusting the angle of the patient's arm during an exercise. Its movements are fluid and precise.
In the background, a human nurse is happily checking a monitor, looking relieved, no longer burdened with constant manual assistance.
The patient gives a confident thumbs-up to the nurse.
Medium shot focusing on the interaction between the robot and the patient. Subtle focus pull from the patient's strained muscle to the robot's gentle, steady grip.
Ensure the background elements match the reference image.
#Rehabilitation, #PrecisionCare, #NurseAssistant, #RobotTherapy, #NaturalLighting, #FluidMotion, #DetailedTexture
```
### 場景三：人性化的情感支持
圖像輸入： 請上傳您醫院病房或休息區的清晰照片作為背景參考圖。
文字提示詞 (Prompt)：
```
Reference the provided image of the hospital room for interior design, window view, and overall cozy atmosphere.
Emotionally resonant. Golden hour lighting casting long, soft shadows.
An elderly patient is sitting comfortably by the window, looking contemplative and peaceful.
The human-like robot is sitting on a chair next to them, gently reading an electronic book aloud (the screen is visible, showing text and a gentle animation).
The elderly patient leans their head slightly towards the robot, a small, peaceful smile on their face.
The robot's digital face shows a calm, attentive expression.
Low-angle, shallow depth of field close-up on the patient's peaceful smile and the robot's attentive 'gaze'. Slow, gentle zoom-in to enhance the intimacy.
The background details should faithfully reflect the reference image.
#Companionship, #ElderlyCare, #EmotionalSupport, #GoldenHour, #Peaceful, #ShallowDepthOfField, #Heartwarming
```
## 結尾特寫與標語 (Closing Shot & Logo)
此部分可以不需圖片參考，因為它是一個概念性的特寫，更注重象徵意義。
文字提示詞 (Prompt)：
```
High-contrast, pristine white background. Minimalist and impactful.
A slow-motion shot (960 FPS style) of a human nurse's hand gently meeting and touching the polished, metallic hand of the human-like robot. A perfect moment of collaboration and trust.
The two hands are perfectly in focus.
Text fades in below the hands: 「科技賦能，溫暖守護。 - 智慧醫院」 (Technology empowers, warmth protects. - Smart Hospital)
Ensure perfect focus and clarity on the hands.
#Collaboration, #Trust, #HumanAndMachine, #SlowMotion, #Minimalist, #Impactful, #PristineWhite
```

特別說明：影片中間有些小片段，我沒有特別寫promt，僅用簡單的中文給sora2產生影片的。另外第一次用我忽略的中英文的一致性，所以片段中中英文夾雜。

## 結論
影片製作門檻大幅降低，每個人都可以嘗試製作，相信網路上會充斥許多ai生成的影片，然後原生原創的價值可能才會被重視。而ai生成的速食影片，看短影音的快餐習慣，會導引我們前往何方呢？我很擔心！這也是我跳脫社群媒體發言的主要原因，重新練習撰寫長文，重構思考模式、閱讀習慣。
